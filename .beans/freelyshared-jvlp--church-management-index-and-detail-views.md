Church management under /superadmin/churches:
- Index: list all churches with columns: name, location, status, member count, listings count, needs count, services count, borrows completed count. Sortable and searchable.
- Show: full church detail page with all stats, list of members (with role badges: admin/member/pending), list of recent activity
- Ability to override 'require_admin_approval' setting from superadmin
- View pending-approval members for any church
