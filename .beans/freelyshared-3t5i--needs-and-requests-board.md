Members can post needs/requests for help or items. Fields: title, description, contact info (phone number or email recommended), status (open/fulfilled). Needs auto-expire after 30 days. Poster manages their own need status. Examples: need help moving, looking for a specific tool, need DIY assistance. Browse page shows all open needs from your church.
