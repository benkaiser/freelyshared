Members can list persistent professional services/skills they offer to their church. Fields: title (e.g. Plumbing, Accounting, Face Painting), description of what they can help with, contact preference (phone number and/or approach at church). Browse page shows all services from your church. This is separate from Needs - services are standing offers of skill availability.
